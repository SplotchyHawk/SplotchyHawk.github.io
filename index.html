Biography

My name is Sebastien Godbout, I am 22 years old, and was born in BC Canada. I have recently graduated from Western University of Ontario. I completed an Honors Specialization in Computer Science and a Minor in Game Development. Using the knowledge I have gained through my education, I have created this website using visual studios. My goal is to show my coding capabilities, personality, and determination to future employers. I wish to hone my skills into a certain subject instead of having a wide variety of knowledge about the software development industry. I am an attentive, ambitious, and resourcful individual seeking to create lasting business relationships. I hope to solidify these connections with the skills I have aquired from my education and individual experience. Python is one of my earliest languages. It acted as my introduction to my bachelors degree. As I progressed through classes, they adobted more indepth languages such as Java, C++, and C. These were the most popular languages throughout my education. I created anything from TikTakTo games against the computer to data management algorithms made to simplify output from external API. I also delved into SQL databases where I managed customers, vehicles, and IDs pertaining to different available bus trips. This incorporated HTML, PHP, and CSS in order to create an interface that allows users to add themselves to the SQL databases and book or cancel trips. On top of this, my gaming minor made me work with C# within Unity. You can find some of my work with unity in the Gaming section of the website.
