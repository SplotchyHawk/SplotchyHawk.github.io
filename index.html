Sebastien Godbout

Biography

My name is Sebastien Godbout, I am 22 years old, and was born in BC Canada. I grew up with an artistic and athletic background. First of all, at the age of five I took up soccer, baseball, and taekwondo. I gravitated towards the endurance and fast pace of the sports which ultimately had me slowly faze out baseball. I went on to get my junior school blackbelt at the age of eleven. This is where I stopped martial arts because getting an international was the next logical step bt required me to be sixteen. this does not take away from how much it taught me in self control, defense, and respect. The physical activity I did stick with long term is soccer. The sport has allowed me to stay in great shape and acts as an escape from the world's trials and tribulations. Furthermore, artistically I have a gift with drawing. All throughout French emersion elementary school I would illustrate comic panels that told a story. These stories, however basic with traces of character development, would be what inspired me to follow my current path through through university. With the help of great friends and in turn great grades and study habbits, I excelled in achademics earning the honor role scholarship my school offered to its students. On top of this I earned an equivalent scholarship in the physical education section of the school.
